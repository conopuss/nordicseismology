Welcome

Welcome to the joint 57th Nordic Seismology Seminar and NordForsk Nordic DAS workshop, hosted by the Icelandic Meteorological Office!

The events will initially start with the Nordic DAS project workshop on June 8th. The 57th Nordic Seismology Seminar will be held from 9–11 June 2026 at Underheimar, Bústaðavegur 7, 105, Reykjavík.

We look forward to seeing you in Reykjavík!
